Why wallcoverings?

Safety

Myth Busters

Myth: Wallpapers peel off and require constant repairs

+
Fact: Low-quality adhesives used by untrained laborers can peel. Vastu Home Decor India uses premium, eco-friendly European adhesives and offers professional installation guarantees. Properly installed wallpaper stays intact for 15+ years.

Myth: Wallpapers are extremely expensive compared to paint

+
Fact: While the initial cost of wallpaper is slightly higher, it lasts 5x longer. You would repaint a room 4-5 times over a 15-year period, making paint 3 times more expensive in the long run.

Myth: Wallpapers capture moisture and attract mold

+
Fact: Our wallpapers are made with breathable, non-woven backing materials that allow moisture to escape, preventing mold growth. If mold does exist under wall surfaces, we pre-treat walls before installation.
